## Ruled out (taken)

- `lehr.haus` — already a live Boston Jewish learning tavern ("A Tavern & House of Learning"). The perfect-sibling name, sadly gone, and a name-collision risk.
- `lehrhaus.de` / `.org` / `.com` — taken.
- `forum.haus`, `salon.haus`, `rede.haus` — taken.
- `lehr.app`, `lehr.de`, `lehr.club`, `frankfurt.club` — taken.

## My recommendation

Pick **`lehr.salon`** if the brand voice is closer to *literary salon / Bildungsbürgertum*. Pick **`denk.forum`** if it's closer to *Habermasian public sphere*. Pick **`denk.haus`** if konzert.haus-sibling visual symmetry trumps everything.
